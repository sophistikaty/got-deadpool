goT Death-pool

You win or you die.
    The Rules:
  • You can choose from 0-10 characters that you think will die.
  • You must submit your choices before the season premiere on Sunday, April 14th.
  • You get 4 points for every character you choose that is dead at the end of the season.
  • You get 1 point for ever character you don't choose that isn't dead at the end of the season.
  • A character that turns into a wight is dead.
  • A character that dies and comes back to life via blood magic (or other means) is still alive.
  • Characters need to be definitively dead to be worth 4 points. Cliffhangers don't count
  • In the event of a tie a winner will be decided with a round of Game of Thrones trivia.
